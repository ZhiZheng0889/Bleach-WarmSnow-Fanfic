# Death Stage 01 — Clash Between Worlds (Arc Guide)

[← Previous](43-transition-2.md)

This arc establishes the *two blueprints* that will collide throughout the Ten Stages of Death.

## Core Throughline

- **White Side (Order)**: stability through **names, courts, and hierarchy**.
- **Black Side (Forge)**: stability through **shared labor, transparent stores, and hard pessimism about human nature**.
- **The seam**: a boundary that becomes natural *only when people stop watching it*.

## Key Terms (quick reference)

- **Spirit King / Soul King**: The same figure, framed differently by perspective.
- **First Smith**: Hellborne founder; anti-feudal, forge-as-politics worldview.
- **Warm Snow**: an omen/catastrophe pattern; heat where cold should be; social collapse pressure-test.
- **Walls vs Struts**: containment vs bracing; denial-of-danger vs admitted-danger.
