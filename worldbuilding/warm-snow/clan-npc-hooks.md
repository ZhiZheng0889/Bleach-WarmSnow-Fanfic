# Clan NPC Hooks

Quick handles for key clan NPCs: motives, leverage, and scene hooks.

- **Xu**
  - *Patriarch Xu Jian*: Motive—profit + suppressor trade with 12th. Leverage—threaten to expose deals to 11th. Hook—caught smuggling Banner Sigils into Rukongai.
  - *“Hammer” Rui*: Motive—test Blood Quench steel. Leverage—needs rare ores. Hook—duels Kenpachi’s men to shatter his own prototypes.
  - *Smuggler Lin*: Motive—feed mimics to gangs. Leverage—river routes via Yue. Hook—double-crosses Yue captain for pardon.

- **Bai**
  - *Matriarch Bai Yulan*: Motive—censor Hollow reports. Leverage—keeps forbidden scrolls. Hook—Byakuya confronts her over erased lineage.
  - *Archivist Han*: Motive—doubts censorship. Leverage—secret copy of Hollow dossier. Hook—offers it to Urahara in exchange for asylum.
  - *Pursuer Mei*: Motive—silence leaks. Leverage—Silence Scrolls. Hook—stalks a Shadow-Walker who stole a mask.

- **Yue**
  - *Captain Yue Zhen*: Motive—tolls/bribes. Leverage—controls ferries. Hook—Soi Fon plants a spy in his checkpoint.
  - *Quartermaster Ruo*: Motive—arm Infinite Arhat couriers. Leverage—Tideway Tokens cache. Hook—tokens go rogue, slicing allies.
  - *Ferryman Jiao*: Motive—illicit Senkaimon runs. Leverage—knows hidden gates. Hook—caught between 2nd Division and Chains Path recruiters.

- **Shen**
  - *Healer Shen Lian*: Motive—public charity mask. Leverage—clinic networks. Hook—Unohana uncovers falsified patient records.
  - *Doctor Wei*: Motive—trial results. Leverage—Verdant Needle data. Hook—sells data to Mayuri; Hollow reiatsu corrupts his kit.
  - *Orderly Rin*: Motive—survive. Leverage—access to Jade Inhalers. Hook—turns sedative into antidote mid-raid.

- **Long**
  - *Commander Long Hao*: Motive—conscription quotas. Leverage—Banner drills. Hook—faces Kenpachi after a banner is torn.
  - *Sergeant Kai*: Motive—brand draft-dodgers. Leverage—Conscription Tags. Hook—tags get spoofed, forcing him to chase flares.
  - *Adjutant Fen*: Motive—noble contracts. Leverage—guard rosters. Hook—secretly leaks routes to 13th to curb Long overreach.
