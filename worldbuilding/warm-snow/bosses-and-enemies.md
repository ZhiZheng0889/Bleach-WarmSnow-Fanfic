# Bosses & Enemies (affiliation map)

Use this to tag boss archetypes to clans/sects and crossover beats.

## Act-aligned bosses (Warm Snow → crossover)
- **Act I – Talisman Bomber**: Backed by Clan Yue smugglers; uses Tideway Tokens and mimic grenades. Counter: 2nd Division, Shadow-Walker disarms.
- **Act II – Gate Captain/Barrier Specialist**: Guard lieutenant influenced by Bai/Long; layers Bai seals over Long banners. Counter: Thunderfury cracks shields; Kido filter from Urahara.
- **Act III – Dangai/Frost Warden**: Bai archivist guarding forbidden scrolls; freezes corridors like ice stacks. Counter: Bitter River Bodhi, Frost Army.
- **Act IV – Hell Forge Overseer**: Xu-funded ritualist running a portable forge; Chains Path escorts. Counter: Chains of Return, Furnace Heart Core interrupts.
- **Act V – Royal Guard Emissary Duel**: Wildcard neutral; tests Hellborne Unique blades; Bai tries to steal notes. Counter: False Moon talisman feints.
- **Hueco Mundo DLC – Arrancar Lord**: Experiments with Shen poisons; corrupts mimics on hit. Counter: Azure Dragon lightning, Wardstone.

## Elite enemies and factions
- **Yue Toll Captains**: control river checkpoints; carry Seal Lanterns purchased from Guard; stash mimics in lanterns.
- **Shen Trial Doctors**: run black clinics; deploy Verdant Needles; guarded by conscripted Long mercs.
- **Bai Censors**: silence witnesses with Sealing Scrolls; travel with Kido Corps defectors.
- **Long Enforcers**: press-gang outer districts; wield Banner Sigils; hire Seven-Sword muscle as intimidators.
- **Xu Forge Retinues**: smith-bodyguards; carry Blood Quench hammers that fuel rage stacks for allies.

## Loot/rescue hooks
- Beating a **Yue bomber** yields a Tideway Token; confiscation risks Bai censorship squads arriving.
- Freeing a **Shen patient** reveals antidote recipes; deliver to 4th Division/Unohana for alliance.
- Stealing a **Bai scroll** unlocks a unique Kido-form; Hellborne wants it to spoof Zanpakuto spirits.
- Cracking a **Xu forge cache** grants a re-roll token (mini Furnace Heart) usable once per arc.
