# Sect Hooks (statue-style)

Quick acquisition/build notes for each sect, with perk/failure flavor for scenes or soft mechanics.

- **Seven-Sword Shura** — Stat focus: melee/rage/armor pen. Acquisition: base statue. Perk: Killing Intent/Bloodbath windows; Failure: overextends into traps if rage starved.
- **Infinite Arhat** — Stat focus: flying sword damage, ranged crit, lightning/frost mix. Acquisition: defeat Sima Ying. Perk: Blade Cage control; Failure: tokens/lanterns short in dry zones.
- **Carousing Acalantha** — Stat focus: burn damage, intoxication synergy, rage gain from drinking. Acquisition: defeat Lu Kunhai. Perk: Demon Wine burn bursts; Failure: flammable; over-intoxication slows reflexes.
- **Thunderfury Vajra** — Stat focus: lightning damage, movement speed, interruption. Acquisition: defeat Qin Wu. Perk: Thunder Prison/ Furious Thunder cracks shields; Failure: drains stamina if overcast.
- **Bitter River Bodhi** — Stat focus: frost damage, freeze duration, control → burst. Acquisition: defeat Zhao Changgeng. Perk: Frost Army shatter setups; Failure: weak in heat/fire zones.
- **Mother Trinity** — Stat focus: bleed/poison stacking, life drain, mobility. Acquisition: kill 9 Cursed Tomes. Perk: Crimson Sea/Parasite attrition; Failure: antidotes flip if Shen medics intervene.
- **Shadow-Walker** — Stat focus: block/parry counters, stealth, riposte damage. Acquisition: buy via Scarlet Treasure (Red Souls). Perk: Shadow Guard/Reversal blowbacks; Failure: silence fields can mute their timing.
- **Soul Blade Shura (DLC2)** — Stat focus: flying sword spirits, summon duration, armor bypass. Acquisition: defeat a DLC2 boss. Perk: Ten Thousand Swords overwhelm; Failure: Zanpakuto spirits resist, causing desync.
- **Wheels of Solar and Lunar (DLC2)** — Stat focus: dual-element burn/freeze, cooldown swaps, tempo disruption. Acquisition: defeat a DLC2 boss. Perk: Duality/Last Eclipse break stalemates; Failure: mistiming flips buffs to debuffs.
