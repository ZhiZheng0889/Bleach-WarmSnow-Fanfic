# Seven-Sword Shura

Melee pressure sect focused on gap-closing and execution windows.

- **Starter skills**: Cold Blood (guaranteed), Flurry, Hidden Strike.
- **Marquee skills**: Killing Intent, Bloodbath.
- **Unlock**: available from start.
- **Hallmarks**: twin blades or greatswords, bloodthirst rites, fearless rushes into elite packs.
- **Gear tilt**: favors melee damage, rage gain, armor pen; Xu forge steel is prized.
- **Crossovers**: rebels against Long conscription; clashes with 11th Division brutes; valued by Xu for testing Blood Quench steel.
