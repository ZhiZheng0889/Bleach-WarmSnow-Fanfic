# Hellborne Path Leaders (build hooks)

Leaders and Vice Leaders for the ten Paths, with quick build notes you can drop into scenes.

- **Blade Path**
  - Leader: *Asahi Rakuyo* — Bleeds his own Reiatsu to sharpen mimics mid-fight; runs a **rage/armor-pen build** with Blood Calyx + Blood Quench steel; specializes in captain duels.
  - Vice: *Murasame Rei* — Mutes Zanpakuto spirits for three heartbeats; **silence/control build** with Sealing Scroll mimics + Banner Sigils to pin targets before silence hits.

- **Frost Path**
  - Leader: *Rime Tsukuyo* — Stacks frost zones like Senkaimon traps; **zone control build** with Frost Arm/Frost Army + Black Tortoise relics.
  - Vice: *Haku Jinpachi* — Fires delayed glaciers that detonate on spiritual pressure spikes; **delayed burst build** with Tricolor Crystal + Chains to hold targets in the blast.

- **Thunder Path**
  - Leader: *Raiden Kisho* — Splices thunder into Shunpo, cracking barriers; **barrier-break build** with Furious Thunder + Azure Dragon set.
  - Vice: *Ikazuchi Nao* — Turns mimic relics into lightning rods to fry Kido nets; **anti-caster build** with Thunder Prison + Seal Lantern hijacks.

- **Poison Path**
  - Leader: *Karasu Benio* — Layered venoms bypass Hollow regen; **DoT/attrition build** with Crimson Sea + Shen brews.
  - Vice: *Sango Kiryu* — Counter-poisons flip toxins into buffs; **support/cleanse build** with Parasite + Jade Inhaler variants.

- **Blood Path**
  - Leader: *Okuro Gen* — Auto-distills spilled blood into rage stacks; **berserker sustain build** with Bloodbath + Blood Calyx.
  - Vice: *Shion Akeboshi* — Siphons captain-level Reiatsu to heal squads; **healer/drain build** with Verdant Needles tuned to siphon, plus Banner Sigils for cover.

- **Flying Swords Path**
  - Leader: *Yatori Kage* — Micro-manages sword swarms; **orbiting DPS build** with Infinity + Blade Cage + Azure Filament Charm.
  - Vice: *Setsuna Rin* — Uses mimic blades as exploding decoys; **trap/harassment build** with Filament Cutouts + Tideway Tokens to bend trajectories.

- **Chains Path**
  - Leader: *Guren Fudo* — Chains as Hellmouth conduits; **control/logistics build** with Chains of Return + Hellmouth seams for reinforcements.
  - Vice: *Haru Natsume* — Locks Zanpakuto releases for a breath; **anti-release build** with suppressor links + Paper tags to stagger releases.

- **Furnace Path**
  - Leader: *Magatetsu Iori* — Mobile forge, re-rolls mimic stats mid-fight; **field engineer build** with Furnace Heart Core + on-the-fly rerolls.
  - Vice: *Riku Ensho* — Overheats enemy weapons until they crack; **disarm build** with overheat sigils + Banner fragments to slow.

- **Paper Talismans Path**
  - Leader: *Suzu Onmyo* — Hides path seals on konso; copies Kido; **seal/deception build** with False Moon Talismans + Kido mimic tags.
  - Vice: *Renpa Meiho* — Fakes spiritual signatures to mask raids; **stealth/infiltration build** with signature fakers + Silence Scroll cases.

- **Specters Path**
  - Leader: *Kageho Shizune* — Projects Wraiths mimicking Zanpakuto spirits; **misdirection build** with afterimage relics + spirit-voice jammers.
  - Vice: *Arata Yomi* — Turns relic mimics into detachable afterimages; **feint/escape build** with Recall Links + Specter afterimages to mislead sensory captains.
