# Hellborne Path Hooks

Quick prompts for Path squads/leaders beyond the builds page.

- **Blade**: Duels captains to test Blood Calyx resonance; will trade suppressor tech for rare ores.
- **Frost**: Freezes Senkaimon frames to reroute traffic; leaves ice “records” Bai wants.
- **Thunder**: Specializes in frying Seal Lanterns and Kido nets; seeks Urahara counter-tech to steal.
- **Poison**: Seeds Shen brews into Rukongai wells; bargains antidotes for captives.
- **Blood**: Harvests battlefield blood to fuel rage engines; offers “healing” siphons to buy loyalty.
- **Flying Swords**: Runs aerial escorts for Yue ferries; Blade Cage used to kidnap scribes.
- **Chains**: Snatches prisoners mid-transport; sells Hellmouth transit to smugglers.
- **Furnace**: Sets up pop-up forges in Dangai pockets; trades rerolls for stolen Bai scrolls.
- **Paper Talismans**: Records Bankai silhouettes; forges false signatures for infiltrations.
- **Specters**: Plants false Zanpakuto voices; scouts captain routes, leaves afterimages as decoys.
